We respect your right to control your personal data. If you would like to delete your QuickFeed account and all associated data, you can do so by following the steps below or by contacting us directly.

HOW TO REQUEST ACCOUNT DELETION

  1. Send us an email from the email address associated with your QuickFeed account to SSandG.com@gmail.com with the subject line: "Delete My QuickFeed Account".
  2. We will verify your identity by confirming the email address matches an existing account in our system.
  3. Your account and data will be deleted within 7 business days of verification. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

WHAT DATA IS DELETED

When you request account deletion, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

Data Type Action
Email address Permanently deleted
Password (hashed) Permanently deleted
Topic preferences Permanently deleted
Account metadata (creation date, last sign-in) Permanently deleted

AI-generated news summaries displayed in the app are not tied to individual user accounts and are not deleted, as they are shared content available to all users.

Please note:

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose access to your account and all personalized settings. If you wish to use QuickFeed again after deletion, you will need to create a new account.

DATA RETENTION

Upon deletion, all personal data is removed immediately from our active databases. We do not retain any personal information after account deletion, unless required by law for fraud prevention or legal compliance purposes, in which case data may be retained for up to 30 days before permanent deletion.

CONTACT US

Email: SSandG.com@gmail.com

Subject line: "Delete My QuickFeed Account"

Response time: Within 7 business days
